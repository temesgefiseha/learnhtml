    • Closing tag:
  • The opening tag and its closing tag together specify a container for the content they enclose

    • Inline tags: no.
  • Not case sensitive

Basic Tags

  • Heading Tags
    • HTML has six levels of headings: <'h1'>, <'h2'>, <'h3'>, <'h4'>, <'h5'>, and <'h6'>.
    • While displaying any heading, browser adds one line before and one line after that heading.
